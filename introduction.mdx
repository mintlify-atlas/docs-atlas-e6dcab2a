# Welcome to SeanceAI

**Step into a digital séance and converse with the greatest minds humanity has ever known.** From Einstein's thought experiments to Cleopatra's political wisdom, from da Vinci's boundless curiosity to Lincoln's moral clarity—experience history through authentic, AI-powered conversations.

<Info>
  SeanceAI isn't just another chatbot. It's a meticulously crafted experience that brings history to life through authentic roleplay, era-appropriate knowledge, and genuine personality.
</Info>

## What Makes SeanceAI Special?

### Authentic Historical Personas

Each historical figure speaks in their unique voice with carefully crafted personalities:

- **Unique personality traits** and speaking styles specific to each figure
- **Era-appropriate knowledge** - figures don't know about events after their death
- **Genuine reactions** to modern concepts (they'll ask you to explain!)
- **Historical accuracy** with references to real experiences, works, and relationships

## Technology Stack

**Backend**
- Python 3.11+ with Flask
- OpenRouter API for multi-model AI access
- Server-Sent Events (SSE) for streaming responses
- Intelligent retry logic with automatic model fallback

**Frontend**
- Vanilla JavaScript (no framework dependencies)
- Modern CSS with custom properties
- LocalStorage for conversation persistence

**AI Models**
- **Swift tier**: Gemma 3, Llama 3.3 70B (free models)
- **Balanced tier**: GPT-4o Mini, Claude 3.5 Haiku, DeepSeek V3
- **Advanced tier**: GPT-4o, Claude Sonnet 4, Gemini 2.5 Pro

<Note>
  This is an educational entertainment tool. While we strive for historical accuracy in personalities and knowledge, the AI responses are generated and should not be used as primary historical sources.
</Note>
